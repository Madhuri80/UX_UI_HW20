REDESIGNING KEEP SANANTONIO BEAUTIFUL

UX/UI Case study

Problem Statement

The Keep SA Beautiful Website is designed to provide information about how to protect the SA environment and give users an opportunity to donate to or volunteer with the organization. However, we have noticed that the website fails to effectively convey these aspects due to broken links, a confusing navigation menu, and poor page layouts. As a result, users experience confusion and frustration while attempting to navigate through the website.

Quantitative Research

Usability Testing

Participants: 5, 30-35 years old, Keep San Antonio Beautiful original Website

Usability Testing Plan Objective

Evaluate the navigation systems and usability heuristics of the website and analyze how users navigate from the home page to efficiently access donation and volunteer information.

Participants felt that the issues that they encountered while going through the website, didn’t make it look professional and trustworthy, and didn’t enticed them to donate or volunteer.

Heuristic & Accessibility Evaluation

we conducted the heuristic evaluation based on appearance and aesthetics, content, navigation, efficiency and functionality. We rated them from 1 to 3. ( 1 being low and 3 being high in fulfilling the requirements.

Jakob Nielsen’s 10 Usability Heuristics- Redline and Annotate

Using Jakob Nielson 10 usability heuristics defined the heuristics issues and redline and annotate them together with the results of our accessibility and usability test to pinpoint and define the major problems of the website.

Hi-Fi Five Second Usability Testing

we wanted to discover first impressions of our Keep San Antonio Beautiful redesign and test how the homepage communicates its purpose and credibility

Users were asked questions related website after looking into our website for 5 seconds.

  1. 1. What are the main elements you can recall?
  2. 2. What do you think is the purpose of this page?
  3. 3. Who do you think the intended audience is?
  4. 4. What was your impression of the design?

Validation and Feedback

Majority of users felt that the website feels inviting , the overall designed looked clean and modern. They complemented our carousel designs and sliders. The Pop- Up on the homepage with donate and volunteer was appreciated. The few valuable users feedback that we want to work on our next steps are:

  1. 1. We want to work on the font size of “Our Mission” Statement
  2. 2. Make other page to make it more interactive
  3. 3. Reinvent the logo to match the overall theme of our style guide
