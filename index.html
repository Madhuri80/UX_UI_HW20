REDESIGNING KEEP SANANTONIO BEAUTIFUL

UX/UI Case study

Problem Statement

The Keep SA Beautiful Website is designed to provide information about how to protect the SA environment and give users an opportunity to donate to or volunteer with the organization. However, we have noticed that the website fails to effectively convey these aspects due to broken links, a confusing navigation menu, and poor page layouts. As a result, users experience confusion and frustration while attempting to navigate through the website.

Usability Testing

Participants: 5, 30-35 years old, Keep San Antonio Beautiful original Website

Usability Testing Plan Objective

Evaluate the navigation systems and usability heuristics of the website and analyze how users navigate from the home page to efficiently access donation and volunteer information.

Findings

Majority of the participants said that the redesigned website was trustworthy, and said that the Mid fidelity design of the website made them feel like they wanted to donate to the organization. The participants were able to complete the tasks but we observed them saying is the ‘programs tab’ the same as volunteer events? Also, got confused in the “Upcoming Events” section, in the homepage and majority of them asked if they could volunteer from the section.
